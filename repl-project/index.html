Surfing

Surfing relieves the stress od a daily life and lets you escape the stress of the world and just be one with the waves. This website is an insight to the secertes of the beaches in southern California. I will explain the many things that you need to know about these beaches and the waves that these beaches have. For example you will need to know where they are located, what board to ride, paddle out distance and time, water depths, and the wave descriptions. There are very unique beaches in southern California, inckuding reef break and shre break which are very different waves and water depths.

On this website I will also be going over not just surfing but body boarding as well. Body boarding and surf are similiar and different they are similiar because they both ride the wave similiar to each other. But they are different as well. For example on a surf board you stand up once you are on the wave, while on the body board you lay on you stomach or on occasion you can ride one your knees. The main differences between the two are the boards used and the position in which you are in. Surfing Description Wiki
